Callaghan Creative

Artistiaid mewn pren a charreg yw Susanna a Phil Callaghan, sydd hefyd yn creu delweddau cyfryngau cymysg haniaethol.
Caiff eu gwaith ei ysbrydoli gan dirweddau a morweddau hardd, a goleuni a gwagle hynod Môn a gogledd Cymru.

Susanna and Phil Callaghan are artists in wood and stone, also creating abstract mixed media images.
Their work is inspired by the beautiful land and seascapes, wildlife and extraordinary light and space of Anglesey and North Wales.
