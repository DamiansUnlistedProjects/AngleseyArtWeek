Caffi Siop Mechell

Mae Caffi Siop Mechell wedi ennill tir ledled yr ynys enw da am ei arddangosfeydd chwarterol o leol artistiaid ochr yn ochr â bwyd rhagorol,
cyfeillgar awyrgylch a siop grefftau.
David Weaver yn dangos casgliad newydd.

Caffi Siop Mechell has gained an island wide reputation for its quarterly exhibitions of local artists alongside excellent food,
a friendly atmosphere and a craft shop.
David Weaver shows a new collection of abstract work.
