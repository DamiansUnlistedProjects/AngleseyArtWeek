Wispy Willow Creations

Dw i’n creu cerfluniau anifeiliaid unigryw mewn helygen ar gyfer yr ardd a’r cartref.
Enghreifftiau o waith anifeiliaid domestig a gwyllt, adar a darnau unigryw pensaerniol. Byddaf yn gweithio ar gomisiwn.
Mae fy ngwaith i’w weld mwn mannau preifat a chyhoeddus, atynfeydd twristaidd a chartrefi mawreddog.

I create bespoke willow animal sculptures for the garden & home.
Domestic & wild animals, birds & architectural statement pieces.
My work is commission based, my sculptures can be seen in private & public spaces, tourist attractions & stately homes.
