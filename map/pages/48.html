ST MWROGS CHURCH, LLANFWROG, HOLYHEAD, LL65 4YL

Cymerwch yr A5025 o’r Fali tuag at Fae Cemaes. Ar ôl mynd trwy Lanfachraeth cymerwch y troad chwith i gyfeiriad Sandy Beach. Mae hen Eglwys Sant Mwrog i’w gweld ar y chwith ar ôl milltir.
Take the A5025 from Valley towards Cemaes Bay. After passing through Llanfachraeth take the left turn signposted Sandy Beach. The former Church of St Mwrogs can be seen on your left after 1 mile.
