Janet & John

Mae gwaith John yn ymgais i egluro’r byd o’i cwmpas.
Mae gwaith Janet yn archwilio bydoedd mewnol ac allanol.
Bydden nhw’n arddangos delweddau wedi’u paentio, printiau, tecstilau, ffotograffau, cerameg a dodrefn.

John’s work is an attempt to explain the world around him.
Janet’s work explores inner and outer worlds.
They will be exhibiting painted images, prints, printed textiles, photographs, ceramics and customised furniture.

SWYN-YR-AFON, LLANBEDRGOCH, LL76 8SQ

Yn Llanbedrogch, trowch i’r gorllewin yn y groesffordd. Mewn 100 metr mae arwydd ceffyl a marchog. Y tŷ nesaf ar y dde, gyda baner Wcrain ydy Swyn-yr-Afon. Mae mynediad i’r stiwdio yng nghefn y tŷ.
Into Llanbedrgoch and at the (staggered) crossroads turn west. 100 metres along there is a horse and rider sign. Next house on the right, with a Ukrainian flag in the garden is Swyn-yr-Afon. Entry to studio is at the back of the property.
