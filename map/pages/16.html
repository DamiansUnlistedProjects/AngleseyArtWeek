Mae Peter Read yn gwneud printiau giclée lliwgar i godi ei galon.
. Ar ôl gweithio ar hyn am ddeng mlynedd bydd detholiad o’i ymdrechion gorau yn cael eu harddangos yn ystod yr Wythnosau Celf.
Mae Peter wedi’i ysbrydoli gan Paul Klee a John Craxton.

Peter Read makes colourful giclée prints to cheer himself up.
He has been working on this for ten years and a selection of his best efforts will be on display during the Arts Weeks.
Peter is inspired by Paul Klee and John Craxton.

TYN Y GAT, GLANRAFON, LLANGOED LL58 8SY

O faes parcio Glanrafon ewch i fyny’r allt. Trowch i’r dde ar yr ynys laswelltog. Pasiwch yn troi i’r Traeth Gwyn. Mae Tyn y Gat yn chweched ty ar hyd y lôn; bwthyn unllawr gwyn. Ewch i mewn trwy’r porth.
From car park at Glanrafon go up hill. Turn right at grassy island. Pass turning to White Beach. Tyn y Gat is sixth house along the lane; a white single storey cottage. Please enter through porch.
