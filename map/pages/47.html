Liz Toole

Mae Liz Toole yn beintiwr a gwneuthurwr printiau llawn amser ac mae ei chariad at adar i’w weld yn amlwg yn ei gwaith.
Mae printiau sgrin a thoriadau leino Liz yn cael eu dylunio a’u hargraffu ganddi hi.
Mae’n dangos ei phaentiadau a’i phrintiau mewn orielau ledled y DU.

Liz Toole is a full time painter and printmaker and it’s clear to see her love of birds.
Liz’s screenprints and linocuts are designed and hand printed by her.
She shows her paintings and prints in galleries all over the UK.
