Bryn Humphreys

Mae fy stiwdio’n meddiannu dwy ystafell yn fy nghartref, sef oriel a gweithdy stiwdio ac mae’n agored yn ystod y Pasg a thrwy drefniant.
Dw i’n paentio tirluniau Môn a Gwynedd yn bennaf mewn acrylig, olew a dyfrlliw a minnau hefyd yn ymgymryd â chomisiynau.

My studio occupies two lower rooms at my home, a gallery and studio workspace and is open during Easter and by appointment.
I primarily paint landscapes of Anglesey and Gwynedd in acrylic, oil and watercolour and I also undertake commissions.
