Marion Rose

Mae Marion yn cael ei denu at yr hen dechnegau argraffeg o engrafiadau, toriad leino a syanotype.
Trwy’r rhain, mae hi’n archwilio tirweddau, bywyd gwyllt a choed Ynys Môn a thu hwnt.

Marion is drawn to the old printmaking techniques of engravings, lino cuts and cyanotype.
Through these, she explores the landscapes, wildlife and woods of Anglesey and beyond.
