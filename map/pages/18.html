Plas Bodfa Projects

Cyflwyno gosodiadau, perfformiadau ac archwiliadau o galchfaen a phowdr calchfaen yn ei wahanol ffurfiau.
Mae Mari Rose Pritchard a Julie Upmeyer yn cyflwyno ‘Void Fraction’, ymchwiliad parhaus i athreiddedd ac amser dwfn.
Gweler ein gwefan am amseroedd perfformiad.

Presenting installations, performances and explorations of limestone and limestone powder in its many forms.
Her watercolour paintings and drawings are a celebration of a love of nature, enriched by living on Anglesey with its breathtaking beauty and diversity of wildlife.

PLAS BODFA, LLANGOED, BEAUMARIS, LL58 8ND

O Fiwmares, cymerwch ffordd yr arfordir tua’r gogledd. Wrth gyrraedd Llangoed, chwiliwch am arwydd melyn a du Plas Bodfa. Trowch i’r chwith. Ewch heibio i’r ysgol gynradd. Dilynwch y ffordd am 3 munud. Mae Plas Bodfa ar y chwith.
From Beaumaris, take the coastal road North. Just on entering Llangoed, look for a yellow/black ‘Plas Bodfa’ sign. Turn left. Pass primary school. Follow road for 3 minutes. Plas Bodfa is on the left.
