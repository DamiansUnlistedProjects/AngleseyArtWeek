Lynne Stuart

Mae paentiadau Lynne mewn acrylig wedi’u hysbrydoli gan y ffurfiau a’r patrymau a geir yn y dirwedd leol.
Maent yn dechrau gyda’r amgylchoedd naturiol ond yn aml mae elfen haniaethol i’r cyfansoddiad ac mae lliw yn arbennig yn chwarae rhan bwysig.

Lynne’s paintings in acrylics are inspired by the forms and patterns found in the local landscape.
They start with the natural surroundings but frequently there is an abstract element to the composition and colour in particular plays an important role.

H’artworks (Anne Snaith)

Peintiadau amlgyfrwng gan gynnwys sidan, dyfrlliw, pastel a batic.
Yn cynnal gwersi a chlybiau yn wythnosol. Mae gweithdai ar gael. Artist preswyl yng Ngwesty’r Bulkeley.

Inspired by her faith and the colour and textures in the landscape, trees and seascapes around Anglesey.
Painting in a wide variety of media including silk, watercolours, pastels, acrylic and batik.
Running classes and clubs weekly, workshops available to book.
