Stiwdio Biwmares

Gofod i artistiaid yng nghanol Biwmares.
Mae’r perchennog a’r artist Sally Fairclough wedi creu oriel sy’n cynnwys arddangosfeydd o gelf gwreiddiol a phrintiau celf gain,
stiwdio a gofod gweithdai, dewis eang o offer celf ar werth a casgliad o gynnyrch mewnol.

A space for artists in the heart of Beaumaris.
Owner and artist Sally Fairclough has created a gallery featuring exhibitions of original art and limited edition prints,
studio and workshop space, a wide selection of art materials for sale and a collection of in-house products.
