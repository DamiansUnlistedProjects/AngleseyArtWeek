David Weaver

Ar ôl amlygu gwaith tirweddol a haniaethol yn ystod y blynyddoedd diwethaf, mae’r ffocws eleni ar bortreadau a ffigurau.
Mae’r rhain naill ai’n cael eu tynnu neu eu paentio o fywyd neu eu hysbrydoli gan brofiadau fel digwyddiadau a cherddoriaeth.

Having highlighted landscape and abstract work in previous years, this year’s focus is on portraits and figures.
These are either drawn or painted from life or inspired by experiences such as events and music.
