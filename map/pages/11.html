Janet Bell

Mae Janet yn canolbwyntio ar newidiadau tymhorol ei chynefin ar Ynys Môn.
Mae’n peintio’n reddfol gan newid cyfansoddiad a lliw wrth weithio ar sawl darn ar y tro.
Mae hi’n cael ei hysbrydoli gan y man a’r lle mae hi’n byw ac yn ei garu.

Janet focuses on the seasonal changes of her environment on Anglesey.
She paints expressively, changing composition and colour as she works on several pieces at a time.
She is inspired by the place and the people she lives and loves.
